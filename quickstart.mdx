Follow these four steps to go from a new account to a published booking page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [app.book2befit.com/sign-up](https://app.book2befit.com/sign-up) and sign up with your email address.

    After confirming your email, you'll be taken through a short onboarding flow to name your organization and set your timezone.

    <Tip>
      Your organization name becomes your public URL slug — e.g. `app.book2befit.com/your-studio-name`.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your location">
    Navigate to **Settings → Location** and fill in the information you'd like to show on the public page for the location you're setting up.

    If you run classes in multiple spaces (e.g. Studio A, Studio B), add them under **Settings → Spaces**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first activity and session">
    1. Go to [**Activities**](https://app.book2befit.com/docs/core-features/activities/) and click **\+ New Activity**. Give it a name (e.g. "HIIT Training", "Pilates"), set a default duration and capacity.
    3. Once the activity is created, toggle **Active** on so it shows up for clients. Open the activity row (or edit the session), then enable the **Active** toggle in the session editor to publish it on your public booking page.
    4. Go to **Sessions** and click **\+ New Session**. Pick your activity, set a date, time, and location.

    <Note>
      Recurring sessions (e.g. every Monday at 9am) are available on the **Growth plan** and above. See [Plan Tiers](/reference/tiers).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share your booking page">
    Your public booking page is live at:

    ```text
    app.book2befit.com/[your-slug]
    ```

    Copy the link from **Settings → Location → Public page** and share it with your clients — or add it to your social media bio, on your website or share it through a messaging app.

    Clients can browse sessions, book, and pay without you creating accounts, but they manage bookings by signing into their client portal (linked from the booking confirmation).
  </Step>
</Steps>
