Your location is the physical place where sessions take place. It has its own address, business hours, closure dates, and public page settings.

Book2befit supports multiple locations on a single account — each with its own settings.

## Creating a location

Go to **Location** in the dashboard and click **+ Add Location**. Enter a name and timezone, then click **Create Location**. You're taken directly to the location's settings tabs.

## Location tabs

### General

Set the display name and timezone. The timezone controls how session times are shown on the public booking page for clients in that area.

### Address

Enter your studio's address using the autocomplete search. The address appears on invoices and on the public booking page.

### Business Hours

Define when your studio is open each day of the week. Business hours are shown on the public page and used to validate session times — you can't schedule sessions outside of business hours.

Toggle individual days off for days you're closed.

### Closures

Add specific closure dates — public holidays, annual shutdowns, or one-off closures. On closure dates, new sessions cannot be created and the public page shows the studio as closed.

<Tip>
  Add closures well in advance so clients aren't surprised by a locked booking page. Existing bookings on a closure date are not automatically cancelled.
</Tip>

### Images

Upload photos of your studio. These appear on your public booking page to give clients a feel for the space.

### Contact

Add a public-facing phone number, email address, and website URL. Shown on the public booking page.

### Branding

Customise the look of your public booking page — header text, hero body copy, and footer message.

<Note>
  Branding customisation requires the **Growth plan** or above. See [Plan Tiers](/reference/tiers).
</Note>

### Public Toggle

Control whether this location's public booking page is live. Toggle it OFF to hide the page without deleting the location — useful during setup or temporary closures.

## Multiple locations

If you run more than one studio, add each as a separate location. Use the **location selector bar** at the top of the Location page to switch between them. Each location has its own:

- Public booking page URL
- Business hours and closure dates
- Spaces and equipment
- Branding settings

## Deleting a location

Click **Delete Location** at the bottom of the General tab and confirm. Sessions and bookings linked to that location are retained in your history.

<Warning>
  Deleting a location removes it from the location selector and makes its sessions no longer accessible from the public booking page. This cannot be undone.
</Warning>
