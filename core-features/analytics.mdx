Book2befit gives you a live view of your studio's performance through the Analytics dashboard and the Reporting tool.

<Note>
  Analytics requires the **Growth plan** or above. Advanced analytics requires **Business** or above. Reporting requires **Enterprise**. See [Plan Tiers](/reference/tiers).
</Note>

## Dashboard overview

The main **Dashboard** at `/dashboard` is available on all plans and shows a real-time snapshot:

| KPI card | Description |
|---|---|
| Total clients | All registered clients in your org |
| Active clients | Clients with at least one booking in the selected period |
| Sessions today | Sessions scheduled for today |
| Bookings today | Bookings made today |
| Checked in today | Clients who attended today |
| Sessions this week | Total sessions in the current week |
| Bookings this week | Total bookings in the current week |

## Analytics (Growth+)

Navigate to **Analytics** for deeper insights. Use the period selector to filter by: **7d · 14d · 30d · 90d · 365d**.

### Charts

| Chart | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Bookings & Sessions Trend | Bookings vs. sessions over time — spot busy periods and growth |
| Top Activities | Which class types are most popular by booking volume |
| Booking Status Distribution | Breakdown of Confirmed · Cancelled · Checked-in · No-show |
| New Client Registrations | Client growth over time |

### KPIs (Growth+)

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Revenue | Sum of all completed payments in the period |
| Avg Revenue per Booking | Total revenue ÷ total bookings |
| Total Bookings | All bookings created in the period |
| Occupancy Rate | `current_bookings / capacity` averaged across sessions |
| Check-ins | Clients who were marked as attended |
| No-shows | Clients who booked but didn't attend |

## Advanced analytics (Business+)

Business plan and above unlocks deeper analysis including:

- Revenue breakdown by payment method (Online vs. At Location)
- Revenue trend charts
- Capacity utilisation by location or space
- Payment method distribution (pie chart)

## Reporting (Enterprise)

Enterprise organizations have access to the **Reporting** tool at `/reporting`, which generates downloadable reports:

- **Sessions report** — full session history with bookings and revenue
- **Client report** — client list with booking counts and lifetime value
- **Revenue report** — financial summary by period, activity, or location

Click **Generate Report**, select the report type and date range, and download as CSV or PDF.

## Exporting data

On any plan, you can export raw data from individual sections:

- **Sessions** → Export Sessions CSV
- **Bookings** → Export CSV
- **Clients** → Export CSV
- **Payments** → Export CSV (via Payments dashboard)

<Accordion title="Is data real-time?">
  Yes — KPI cards and charts reflect live data from the database. There is no caching delay.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I see analytics for a specific location?">
  Location-level filtering is available in the Advanced Analytics section (Business+). On Growth, analytics are aggregated across all locations.
</Accordion>
