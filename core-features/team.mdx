Book2befit supports multiple team members with distinct roles. Each role controls what a person can see and do inside the dashboard.

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full access. Manages billing, settings, team, and all data. Only one owner per organization. |
| **Admin** | Same as Owner except cannot manage billing or delete the organization. |
| **Coach** | Views their own schedule, can check in clients, and (if enabled) create sessions. Cannot access payments, settings, or client management. |

## Inviting a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team">
    Navigate to **Team** in the dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click + Invite Member">
    Enter the team member's email address and select their role (Admin or Coach).
  </Step>
  <Step title="They accept the invite">
    The invitee receives an email with a link to accept. Once accepted, they appear in your team list with their assigned role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Invite links expire after 7 days. If a team member doesn't receive the email, check their spam folder or resend the invite from the Team page.
</Tip>

## Coach schedule view

Coaches have access to **My Schedule** — a personal view of only the sessions assigned to them. They can:

- See upcoming sessions and booked clients
- Check clients in or mark no-shows
- Create sessions (if the org setting is enabled)

Coaches do **not** see other coaches' sessions, client contact details, payment data, or settings.

## Enabling coach session creation

By default, coaches cannot create new sessions. To change this:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Toggle **Allow coaches to create sessions** ON.

Coaches can create sessions but cannot delete them — that remains Owner/Admin only.

## Removing a team member

Open the **Team** page, find the member, and click **Remove**. Their account is not deleted — they simply lose access to your organization. If they have another organization, they retain access to that.

## Role permission matrix

| Feature | Owner | Admin | Coach |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dashboard & analytics | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Sessions (view) | ✓ | ✓ | Own only |
| Sessions (create) | ✓ | ✓ | If enabled |
| Sessions (delete) | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Bookings | ✓ | ✓ | View only |
| Clients | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Payments & invoices | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Products | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Team management | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Settings | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Billing & plan | ✓ | — | — |

<Accordion title="Can a Coach be promoted to Admin?">
  Not directly — you'd need to remove the team member and re-invite them with the Admin role. Role changes are not editable in place.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to sessions if I remove a coach?">
  Their sessions remain scheduled and visible in the dashboard. You can reassign the sessions to another coach from the session's edit panel.
</Accordion>
