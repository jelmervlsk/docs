Every Book2befit studio gets a public booking page at:

```
app.book2befit.com/[your-slug]
```

Clients can browse sessions, book, pay online, manage their own bookings, and purchase memberships — no app to download, no login required to browse.

## What clients can do

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Browse & book sessions" icon="calendar-check">
    View all upcoming sessions with availability. Book with one click. Full sessions show a **Join Waitlist** option.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pay online" icon="credit-card">
    Pay by card (or local payment methods via Stripe) at the time of booking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage their bookings" icon="list-check">
    View upcoming and past bookings, download calendar invites, and cancel within your cancellation policy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Buy memberships & packs" icon="tag">
    Purchase products (class packs, memberships) directly from the public portal.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Finding your public URL

Your slug is set during onboarding. To find or copy your full public URL:

1. Go to **Settings → Location**.
2. Copy the **Public page** link.

Share it anywhere — website, Instagram bio, WhatsApp, email signature.

## Booking flow (client perspective)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Client visits your page">
    They see a list of upcoming sessions with activity name, coach, time, location, price, and spots remaining.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Client selects a session and clicks Book">
    If they're not logged in, they're prompted to create a free account or sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Client pays (if session has a price)">
    They complete payment via Stripe. A confirmation email is sent immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Client receives confirmation">
    The confirmation email includes session details, a calendar file attachment, and a link to cancel if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Waitlist

When a session is full, clients see a **Join Waitlist** button. They enter their name, email, and phone number. When a spot opens up (via cancellation or manual promotion from your dashboard), they're automatically moved to a confirmed booking and notified.

Clients can also join a waitlist directly at:

```
app.book2befit.com/[slug]/waitlist
```

## Calendar export

Clients can export their bookings to Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or any `.ics`-compatible app from their bookings page at:

```
app.book2befit.com/[slug]/my-bookings
```

Your studio's session calendar can also be exported as an `.ics` feed for coaches and clients to subscribe to.

## Products on the public page

If you have products enabled (Growth plan or above), clients can browse and purchase them at:

```
app.book2befit.com/[slug]/products
```

This includes one-time class packs, recurring memberships, and any other products you've published.

<Note>
  Products on the public portal require the **Growth plan** or above. See [Plan Tiers](/reference/tiers).
</Note>

## Client accounts

Clients create their account on your public page — not through a separate app. Their account is scoped to your studio. They can update their profile at:

```
app.book2befit.com/[slug]/account
```

## Feedback

After a session, clients can leave a star rating and comment. This appears in your **Feedback** dashboard and helps you track satisfaction over time.

The feedback link is sent automatically in the post-session email:

```
app.book2befit.com/[slug]/feedback
```

<Accordion title="Can I customise the look of my public page?">
  Yes — on the **Growth plan** and above, you can customise the header, hero text, and footer branding from **Settings → Branding**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can clients book without creating an account?">
  No — an account is required to complete a booking. Browsing sessions is public and requires no login.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I hide a session from the public page?">
  Toggle **Allow public booking** OFF on the session. The session remains in your dashboard but won't appear on the public booking page.
</Accordion>
