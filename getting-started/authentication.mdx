## Creating an account

Go to [app.book2befit.com/auth](https://app.book2befit.com/auth) and click **Create account**.

- Email and password are required. Your password should have at least a minimum of 8 characters containing lowercase, uppercase letters, digits and symbols.
- After submitting, you'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link to verify your address.
- Once verified, you're redirected to the onboarding wizard to set up your organization.

<Note>
  If you sign up with an email that already has an account, you'll see: _"You already have an account. Please sign in instead."_
</Note>

## Signing in

Navigate to [book2befit.com](http://book2befit.com) and click on "[Sign in](https://app.book2befit.com/auth)" or go directly to [app.book2befit.com/auth](https://app.book2befit.com/auth) and enter your email and password.

- **Organization owners and admins** are redirected to the dashboard after login.
- **Coaches** are redirected to the dashboard (not the onboarding wizard).
- If you haven't set up your organization yet, you'll be taken to the onboarding wizard.

## Forgot password

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the sign-in page.
2. Enter your email address and submit.
3. You'll receive a password reset email on the registered email address.
4. Click the link in the email and set a new password.
5. Reset links expire after a short period. If the link is already used or expired, you'll see an error and can request a new one.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA / MFA)

2FA adds a second layer of security using a TOTP authenticator app (e.g. Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password).

### Enabling 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Security">
    Navigate to **Settings → Security** in the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Click **Enable 2FA**. A QR code is shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan with your authenticator app">
    Open your authenticator app, scan the QR code, and enter the 6-digit code to verify.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmed">
    2FA status updates to **Active**. You'll now be prompted for a code on every login.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Logging in with 2FA

After entering your password, a second screen prompts for your TOTP code. Enter the current 6-digit code from your authenticator app. If the code is wrong, login is blocked.

### Disabling 2FA

Go to **Settings → Security** and click **Disable 2FA**. After disabling, sign out and back in to confirm MFA is no longer required.

<Warning>
  If you lose access to your authenticator app, contact support to recover your account. There are no backup codes at this time.
</Warning>

## Team member sign-in

Coaches and admins sign in at the same URL. They are not taken through the onboarding wizard — they land directly on the dashboard with their role-limited view.

If a team member hasn't accepted their invite yet, they won't be able to sign in under your organization. See [Team & Roles](/core-features/team) for the invite flow.
