After confirming your email, you're taken through the onboarding wizard at `/onboarding`. This is a one-time setup that creates your organization.

## Step 1 — Company & Contact

Fill in your studio's details:

| Field               | Required | Notes                                                        |
| ------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Company name        | ✓        | Becomes your organization's display name                     |
| VAT number          | —        | Optional, appears on invoices                                |
| Registration number | —        | Optional                                                     |
| Phone               | —        | Contact number for your studio                               |
| Notes               | —        | Internal notes                                               |
| Address             | ✓        | Use the address autocomplete — fields populate automatically |

### Billing address

By default, the billing address is the same as your company address. Toggle **Billing address same as company address** OFF to enter a separate billing address. Toggle it back ON to hide those fields.

## Step 2 — Review

Review your details before submitting. Use **← Previous Step** to go back and make changes. When everything looks correct, click **Create Organization**.

<Tip>
  You can update all of these details later from **Settings → Location** and **Settings → Organization** — nothing is locked after onboarding.
</Tip>

## Joining as a team member

If you were invited to join an existing organization, you don't go through this wizard. Instead:

1. Click the invite link in your email.
2. Create your account (or sign in if you already have one).
3. You're added to the organization with the role assigned by the owner.
4. On login, you land directly on the dashboard — not the onboarding wizard.
