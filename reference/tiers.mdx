## Tier gates in the app

Features locked to a higher tier show an upgrade prompt rather than an error. For example:

- On **Starter**: the **+ Recurring Sessions** button is hidden on the Sessions page.
- On **Starter**: navigating to `/products` shows an upgrade prompt instead of your product list.
- On **Starter**: the **Branding** tab in Settings is not available.

<Note>
  Tier gates are enforced at the API level — not just in the UI. Calls to gated features from lower-tier accounts return a permissions error.
</Note>

## Changing your plan

Go to **Settings → Billing** to upgrade or downgrade your plan. Changes take effect immediately on upgrade. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
  Yes — new organizations start on a free trial that includes Growth-level features. No credit card required to start.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my data if I downgrade?">
  Your data is never deleted on a downgrade. Features locked to your previous tier become read-only — existing recurring sessions remain visible but you cannot create new ones until you upgrade again.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is annual billing available?">
  Yes — annual billing is available and offers a discount compared to monthly. Switch between monthly and annual in **Settings → Billing**.
</Accordion>
