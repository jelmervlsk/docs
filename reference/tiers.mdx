## Tier gates in the app

Features locked to a higher tier show an upgrade prompt rather than an error. For example:

- On **Starter**: the **\+ Recurring Sessions** button is hidden on the Sessions page.
- On **Starter**: navigating to `/products` shows an upgrade prompt instead of your product list.
- On **Starter**: the **Branding** tab in Location settings is not available.

## Changing your plan

Go to **Settings → Billing** to upgrade or downgrade your plan. Changes take effect immediately on upgrade. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

<Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
  Yes — Book2befit has a permanent free plan (Starter). No trial period, no credit card required. You can run your studio on the Starter plan indefinitely and upgrade when you need more features.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my data if I downgrade?">
  Your data is never deleted on a downgrade. Gated features simply show an upgrade prompt — you lose the ability to use them, but nothing is removed. If you're unsure what a downgrade affects, contact [support](mailto:support@book2befit.com) before making the change.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is annual billing available?">
  Yes — annual billing is available and offers a discount compared to monthly. Switch between monthly and annual in **Settings → Billing**. View our pricing plans here: https://book2befit.com/#pricing.
</Accordion>
